关于我 / PROFILE

Yinzi Gong [Portrait from PDF Page 1]

作为一位城市设计和建筑师，在十一年的世界五百强外资企业（AECOM 等）工作经验中，我深度参与了城市总体规划、新城设计、产业园区规划、旅游小镇、休闲度假区、建筑概念设计等多种尺度的规划与建筑设计项目。

长期的全球化项目经历，使我积累了极其丰富的跨国、跨团队协作经验。我擅长将宏观的可持续性生态框架与中微观极富雕塑感的空间形态融合，通过精密合理的空间形态推敲与最新的人工智能造型渲染技术，为城市的可持续发展提供具备高度落地方案的顶层空间设计。

As an associate urban designer and architect with over ten years of trans-regional & cross-team working experience in an international Fortune Global 500 company (such as AECOM), I have deeply participated in various planning and design projects across different scales, including urban master planning, new town design, industrial park planning, tourist towns, leisure resorts, and conceptual architectural design.

With extensive global project experience, I excel in synthesizing macro-scale sustainable ecological frameworks with micro-scale sculptural spatial forms. Through precise spatial form-finding and cutting-edge generative AI rendering technologies, I deliver highly feasible top-tier spatial designs for sustainable urban development.
